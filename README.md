# Pen Warriors - Cyberpunk Arena

**"The classroom is gone, but the war remains."**

## 🖊️ The Legacy

Pen Fight (or Pen War) is the ancient, time-honored method of killing time during boring lectures, perfected by Gen Z students during their school years. It was a test of dexterity, strategy, and the structural integrity of your stationery.

As developers belonging to the last generation of Gen Z, we watched as tablets replaced notebooks and styluses replaced gel pens. Fearing this sacred art form would be lost to history, we built **Pen Warriors** to bring the tactile thrill of knocking your friend's pen off the desk into the digital realm—preserving the tradition for eternity.

## 🎮 How to Play

1.  **Open the Game**: simply open `pen_warriors.html` in any modern web browser.
2.  **The Objective**: Defeat your opponent by either:
    *   **Ring Out**: Knocking them off the table (the arena bounds).
    *   **Destruction**: Depleting their Stability (HP) to 0% via high-velocity impacts.
3.  **Controls**:
    *   **Aim**: Move your mouse cursor around the arena.
    *   **Charge**: Click and **HOLD** the Left Mouse Button (or the "HOLD" button) to build power.
    *   **Fire**: Release the button to flick your pen.
    *   **Ability**: Press **SPACEBAR** to activate your class-specific special move.

## ⚔️ Classes

Choose your weapon wisely. Each pen has unique physics properties:

*   **Gel-Viper (Balanced)**
    *   *Stats*: Average Mass, Average Speed.
    *   *Ability*: **Spike** (Bonus damage on impact).
*   **The Titan (Heavy)**
    *   *Stats*: High Mass, Low Speed, High Knockback resistance.
    *   *Ability*: **Shield** (Nullifies incoming damage).
*   **Neon Ghost (Light)**
    *   *Stats*: Low Mass, High Speed, Low Friction.
    *   *Ability*: **Speed** (Massive velocity boost).
*   **Integra Blue (Striker)**
    *   *Stats*: Tuned for impact absorption.
    *   *Ability*: **Shock Absorber** (Increases mass momentarily to anchor against hits).

## 🛠️ Features

*   **Physics Engine**: Realistic elastic collisions, friction, and wall bounces.
*   **Cyberpunk Aesthetic**: Neon glow effects, dynamic lighting, and synth-style visuals.
*   **Battle Log**: Real-time text commentary of the fight.
*   **Instant Replay**: Watch a slow-motion replay of the last strike.
*   **Local Multiplayer**: Turn-based combat on a single screen.
